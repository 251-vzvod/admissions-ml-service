## 7. Gaps In Current Candidate Set
- which archetypes are underrepresented
- Sustained builder/founder profiles with external proof of execution are underrepresented; many initiatives stay at school or neighborhood scale.
- Clear negative-control profiles with strong evidence but genuinely low motivation are underrepresented relative to polished generic cases.
- Arts-first and vocational/manual-work profiles exist, but they are thinner than STEM, civic, and tutoring-centered archetypes.
- which modalities are underrepresented
- video_interview_transcript_text is missing in 52/52 profiles
- video_presentation_transcript_text is missing in 52/52 profiles
- documents / attachments / portfolio_links are present in 0/52 profiles

## 8. Recommendation
- what to keep
Keep the grounded low-polish / high-upside files and the strong-growth profiles as the most useful evaluation substrate.
- what to rewrite
Rewrite inconsistent files and polished generic files where initiative is mostly rhetorical or outcome-free.
- what to add next
Add video-based modalities, portfolio proof, and more diverse archetypes with sustained non-STEM execution and sharper negative controls.
